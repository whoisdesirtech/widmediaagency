Vendor

Agency reviews uploaded documents for compliance. Vendor is notified of any issues.

Agency creates a Statement of Work: rate, payment schedule, deliverables, and terms.

Admin

Agency reviews and approves the SOW. Contract assembly is blocked until this step.

Gate
7
📑

Contract Assembly

System merges Master Agreement + Role Addendum + SOW into a single contract.

Auto

Both parties review and digitally sign. Contract status becomes "Active."

Both

Vendor

Agency reviews and processes payment according to schedule and terms.

Admin
