Vendor Onboarding & Payment Phases

WhoIsDésir® Media — Freelancer Talent Agreement System

Onboarding

1
🤝

Vendor Registration

Agency adds the contractor to the system with name, business entity, role, and jurisdiction.

Admin Action
2
🔑

Portal Access Granted

Admin generates login credentials using the vendor's real email. Credentials are shared privately.

Admin Action
3
📄

Document Upload

Vendor uploads required documents: Tax form (W-9/W-8BEN), insurance proof, and licensing.

Vendor Action
4
✅

Document Review

Agency reviews uploaded documents for compliance. Vendor is notified of any issues.

Admin Action

Engagement Setup

5
📝

SOW Creation

Agency creates a Statement of Work: rate type, payment schedule, deliverables, equipment, and software.

Admin Action
6
👍

SOW Approval

Agency reviews and approves the SOW. Contract assembly is blocked until approval is given.

Required Gate
7
📑

Contract Assembly

System merges Master Agreement + Role Addendum + SOW into a single reviewable contract.

Automated
8
✍️

Contract Signing

Both parties review and digitally sign the assembled contract. Status becomes "Active."

Both Parties

Payment & Lifecycle

9
🚀

Active Engagement

Work begins under the agreed SOW terms. Vendor delivers against defined milestones and deliverables.

In Progress
10
💰

Invoicing

Vendor submits invoices per the payment schedule (weekly, biweekly, monthly, net 15, or net 30).

Vendor Action
11
🏦

Payment Processing

Agency reviews and processes payment according to the agreed schedule and terms.

Admin Action
12
📊

Contract Renewal

At engagement end, agency can renew with updated SOW terms or mark the contract as completed.

Admin Action
Note: All contracts are drafts until reviewed by a licensed attorney. IP assignment, indemnity, and worker-classification clauses carry real legal risk.
