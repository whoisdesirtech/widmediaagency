3 Viewing Your Statement of Work (SOW)

Your SOW defines your rate, payment schedule, and deliverables. Once your agency admin creates it, you'll see it on your dashboard.

1

Go to your Dashboard

Click Dashboard in the left sidebar

2

Scroll to "My Statements of Work"

You'll see a list of your SOWs with the following details:

3

Review the SOW details

Each SOW shows:
• Rate Type — How you're compensated (hourly, full day, project, etc.)
• Rate — Your agreed dollar amount
• Start Date — When the engagement begins
• Payment Schedule — How often you'll be paid (weekly, biweekly, monthly, net 15, net 30)
• Status — Draft, Approved, Active, or Completed

SOW Statuses Explained
Draft — Your agency admin is still preparing this. Not final yet.
Approved — The agency has approved the SOW. Your contract will be generated next.
Active — The SOW is in effect and work is underway.
Completed — The engagement has ended.
4

Wait for SOW Approval

Once your agency approves the SOW, a contract will be assembled for you to review and sign. You'll see a notification on your dashboard when it's ready.

4 Uploading Your Documents

Before your contract can be finalized, you need to upload required documentation.

1

Click "Onboarding" in the sidebar

Navigate to /contractor/onboarding

2

Upload your Tax Form (Required)

Select W-9 (if you're a U.S. resident) or W-8BEN (if international). Click the upload area and select your completed file. Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG.

3

Upload Insurance Proof (if applicable)

Required if you do on-location photography. Upload your general liability insurance certificate.

4

Upload Licensing Proof (if applicable)

Required for specialized work (drone photography, etc.). Upload your relevant licenses.

Tip: Once all required documents are uploaded, you'll see a green confirmation banner. Your agency admin will review them and your contract will be generated.

5 Reviewing & Signing Your Contract

After your SOW is approved and your documents are uploaded, your agency assembles the contract. You'll see it in your portal.

1

Click "My Contracts" in the sidebar

Navigate to /contractor/contracts

2

Select the contract from the left panel

Click on the contract entry to load the full preview on the right.

3

Read the full agreement

The contract contains:
• Master Agreement — Standard legal clauses (IP rights, confidentiality, indemnity, etc.)
• Your Information — Name, business, role, jurisdiction
• Statement of Work — Your rate, schedule, and deliverables
• Role-Specific Addendum — Photography-specific terms (equipment, licensing, usage rights)

Important: Read the entire contract carefully before signing. This is a legally binding agreement. If you have questions, ask your agency admin before signing.
4

Draw your signature

Scroll to "Your Signature" at the bottom. Use your mouse or finger to draw your signature in the box.

• Click "Clear" if you want to redo it
• Click "Sign" when you're satisfied

5

Done!

Once both you and the agency have signed, the contract status changes to "Active". Your agreement is now fully executed.

Need a copy? Click the "Download" button on the contract preview to save an HTML copy of your signed agreement.

6 Your Onboarding Checklist

Complete these steps to get started:

  • Log in to the contractor portal
  • Upload your W-9 (or W-8BEN if international)
  • Upload insurance proof (if doing on-location work)
  • Review your Statement of Work on the dashboard
  • Wait for your agency to approve the SOW
  • Review the assembled contract in "My Contracts"
  • Sign the contract
  • Download a copy for your records

7 Need Help?

If you have any issues with the portal or questions about your agreement:

  • Login issues? Contact your agency admin to reset your password
  • Contract questions? Ask your agency admin before signing
  • Document upload issues? Make files are PDF, JPG, or PNG and under 10MB
  • General questions? Reach out to your agency contact directly
